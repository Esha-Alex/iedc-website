IEDC Club Briefing

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell (IEDC) at Mar Athanasius College of Engineering is dedicated to fostering a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship among students. We provide a platform for aspiring entrepreneurs to transform their ideas into successful ventures through mentorship, resources, and networking opportunities.

Joining IEDC is simple! Attend our orientation sessions at the beginning of each semester, or contact us directly through the Contact Us section. Membership is open to all MACE students passionate about innovation and entrepreneurship.
